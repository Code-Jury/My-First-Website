LITERATURE

Literature is gotten from the word, literate which means the ability to read and write. Literature is simply defined as creative and imaginative writings. A literature writer is a litterateur while a group of people interested in literature is called literati. Types of Literature FICTION This is an imaginative writing created with the aim of eliciting literary reactions from readers. The people and events are not real but they could be influenced by real events e.g Things Fall Apart by Chinua Achebe. Anchor Tutorial NON-FICTION This is creative writing based on real facts or events. They are different from non-fiction because the people and the events are real. They could be books, articles, journals, novels, plays, poems etc. An example is Why We Struck by Ademola Ademoyega. Anchor Tutorial Sources of Literature * Folklore - traditional stories * Folk dance and music - designed to tell a story about a particular area. * Festival - special occasions to celebrate culture and tradition of the people. * Ritual - an important ceremony performed for religious or social purposes. * Myth - An untrue story meant to explain historical or natural events. * Legend - An old well known story that tells the adventures of great people. * Oral poetry - praise poem or panegyric.
